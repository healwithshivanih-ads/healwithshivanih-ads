E · What we're asking Acumen for

  1. Package price per tier — fill the blanks in Section C.
  2. Bring the FM Base discount in line with your retail packages. Your packages average ~58% off catalogue (Platinum/Elite Gold 67–68%); the FM Base quote is only 32% off. As a recurring B2B partner, match at least your standard package discount — at 58% this base is ~₹7,700.
  3. Combine FM Base + PCOD as one panel for women <45 and strip the duplicated tests (CBC, thyroid, insulin, CRP, blood sugar) — price it as a single combined panel, not the sum of two.
  4. Tier 1 to build on Total Health Platinum (₹2,999), not the ₹12,500 base — confirm the 4 add-ons (insulin, free T3/T4, hs-CRP, homocysteine) at a fair bundled rate.
  5. Hormone add-ons to market rate — current quotes run ~2× (AMH ₹2,350 vs market ₹910, prolactin ₹700 vs ₹350, etc.). This is the main lever on Tiers 2 & 3.
  6. Rev-share on add-ons, not just the base — confirm 25% applies across the board.
  7. Add RBC Folate + RBC Magnesium into scope (currently excluded from the base) — needed for the FM panel.
  8. Home sample collection + digital reports included.
