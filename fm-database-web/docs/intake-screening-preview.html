Beighton hypermobility screen 5 questions

Self-scored by client (out of 5). Coach re-checks bilateral (out of 9) on the Zoom call using the same images. Score isn't shown to client (avoids self-diagnosis) but populates the SOAP Objective block.

1. Pinky finger bends back past 90°
Hold your hand flat (palm down). With the other hand, lift your little finger backwards. Can you bend it past a 90° (right) angle, easily and without pain? Either hand counts.
2. Thumb touches inside of forearm
Bend your wrist down and use the other hand to push your thumb towards the inside of your forearm. Can it reach (or come within a finger's width)? Either thumb counts.
3. Elbow bends backwards past straight
Stretch your arm out straight and have someone take a side photo (or use a mirror). If the elbow visibly curves back past straight by more than ~10°, tick this. Either arm counts.
4. Knee bends backwards past straight
Stand sideways to a mirror or have someone take a side photo. Lock your knee. If it visibly curves back past straight by more than ~10°, tick this. Either knee counts.
5. Palms flat on the floor with knees straight
Stand with feet together. Bend forward from the hips. With knees completely locked straight (don't bend them at all), can you place both palms flat on the floor? Only tick if knees stay 100% straight.

NASA 10-minute lean test conditional

Only asks the HR-measurement version if client has a device. Otherwise: symptom-only screening.

Lean position: head + shoulders touching the wall, heels ~15 cm from the wall
Hold this position for 10 minutes (or sit if you feel close to fainting). Don't fidget or shift weight — let your body relax against the wall.

Form flow — device-conditional

Q1 (always asked):
  "Do you have any of these to measure heart rate?"
    ☐ Smartwatch (Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Whoop)
    ☐ Smart ring (Oura, Ultrahuman, Ringconn)
    ☐ Home blood-pressure monitor (most cuffs show HR too)
    ☐ Fingertip pulse oximeter
    ☐ Chest strap (Polar / Wahoo)
    ☐ Phone app (less accurate, but I'll use one)
    ☐ None of the above

IF none ticked  →  show symptom-only block:
  "When you stand for 5+ minutes (queues, kitchen, lift),
   tick anything you've noticed:"
    ☐ Lightheaded / dizzy
    ☐ Vision tunnelling or going dark
    ☐ Heart racing or pounding
    ☐ Brain fog / hard to think
    ☐ Hot / flushed / sweaty
    ☐ Cold / clammy
    ☐ Nauseous
    ☐ Had to sit down
    ☐ Felt completely fine

IF any device ticked  →  show full HR-measurement block:
  "Quick self-check — 12 minutes total:
   1. Lie down 5 minutes. Note your HR: ___ bpm
   2. Stand against a wall, heels 15 cm from wall, head + shoulders
      touching wall. Stay 10 minutes (or sit earlier if needed).
   3. HR after standing: ___ bpm
   4. Symptoms during the 10 minutes:  [same chip list as above]"

  Coach side: delta-HR ≥ 30 + symptoms = POTS pattern flag.
